BudgetBuddy Security Standard v3.2

All customer data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
Backups are taken every 6 hours. Recovery Point Objective (RPO) is 6 hours and Recovery Time Objective (RTO) is 2 hours.
The incident response team must notify affected enterprise customers within 24 hours of confirming a high-severity incident.
BudgetBuddy maps controls to SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001.
